Here's a simple **Kaun Banega Crorepati (KBC)** game implemented in HTML. You can play it directly in your browser:

  • The game displays a question along with multiple options.
  • Click on the option you think is the correct answer.
  • If your answer is correct, you win money (virtual, of course!).
  • Keep answering questions to become a Crorepati!
  • You can use lifelines to help you answer questions.

Feel free to add more questions to the `questions` array in the script to make the game more interesting. Enjoy playing!
