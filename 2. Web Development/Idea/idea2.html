Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about TodayTrain.

How can TodayTrain guarantee confirmed tickets for same-day travel?
TodayTrain uses a combination of real-time data monitoring, AI predictions, and smart routing algorithms. We scan all available trains, including those from nearby stations, monitor for cancellations, leverage VIKALP scheme, and sometimes suggest split journeys to ensure you get confirmed seats even on the same day.
What if no confirmed tickets are available for my route today?
In the rare case where no confirmed tickets are available through any of our methods, we'll suggest alternative transportation options like buses or flights. We can also notify you instantly if seats become available due to cancellations.
How does the split journey option work?
Split journey combines two or more shorter train rides with confirmed availability instead of one direct train. For example, if Delhi to Mumbai has no confirmed seats, we might suggest Delhi to Kota and then Kota to Mumbai, both with confirmed seats. Our system ensures reasonable connection times between trains.
Is TodayTrain an official IRCTC partner?
Yes, TodayTrain is an authorized B2B partner with IRCTC. We use official APIs to access real-time data and process bookings. All tickets booked through our platform are official IRCTC tickets.
How accurate is the AI prediction for ticket confirmation?
Our AI prediction models have been trained on millions of historical booking patterns and have an accuracy rate of over 94%. The model considers factors like day of week, season, festivals, train route popularity, and historical cancellation patterns to make its predictions.
